Fauneroes is an Indie Tactical-RPG taking place in a fantasy forest world populated with cute animals. Explore the world with endless charming stories, recruit new allies, and overcome tactical challenges.

The game is currently in development, but the demo is already playable for free (about 1h30 long).

  • Live a big adventure full of humor.
  • Cute wildlife animals in armor.
  • Create your team of adorable animals in armor and help them level up.
  • Powerfull combos and strategies for you to figure out, with near infinite combinations of units, species, skills and weapons.
  • Complete map editor & cutscene editor to create your own stories, or discover endless community made stories.

What is Fauneroes?

It's a Tactical-RPG indie game with cute knight animals. It will feature community made content, in an attempt to build a large and diverse world.

How can I play it?

You can play the demo for free on Steam
Also available on Itch.io
The game is still under development. We are aiming at a Kickstarter campaign in late 2024, for a release probably mid 2025.

Will the game have multiplayer?

Yes, multiplayer is planned for co-op. Number of players is tbd, but it will probably be 2 or 3.

Is the game available in French/Spanish/German etc. ?

Not yet as it's a lot of work. The demo will be translated before starting to work on the full game.

What are the targeted platforms?

PC only for the moment.

What shape will take community made content in Fauneroes?

A complete map editor and cutscene editor is available in game. You will be able to create your own stories, and share it with others in game. The best maps will be featured in community events, or even added permanently to the world map as new locations.

How can I support the game?

Whishlist on Steam, rate the demo, follow us on social media and talk about the game around you! We need a large enough following to make the Kickstarter campaign possible.
